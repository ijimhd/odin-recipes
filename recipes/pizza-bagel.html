Mini Bagel Pizzas

Home

Description

These mini pizza bagels with pepperoni and cheese are a yummy homemade snack or easy dinner.

Ingredients

  • 8 mini bagels, split
  • ¼ cup pizza sauce
  • ⅓ cup shredded pizza cheese blend
  • 16 slices turkey pepperoni (such as Hormel)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Arrange bagels, cut-side up, on the prepared baking sheet. Spoon a thin layer of pizza sauce over each bagel half and sprinkle with pizza cheese. Place two pepperoni slices onto each bagel half.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and pepperoni is lightly browned, about 6 minutes. Let cool slightly before serving.
